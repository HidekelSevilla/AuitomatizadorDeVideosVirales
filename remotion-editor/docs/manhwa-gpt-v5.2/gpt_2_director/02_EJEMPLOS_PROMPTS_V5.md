# Ejemplos de prompts V5.2

Los ejemplos demuestran mecánica, no contenido para copiar.

## 1. Base de personaje

```text
Character identity reference sheet of [NAME]. Exactly one Korean [age/role], [build], [face/hair/eyes], neutral relaxed expression. Full body from hair to soles, orthographic front eye-level view, minimal perspective, both open empty hands visible beside the hips, both feet visible. Clean dry [simple base clothing]. Even soft studio illumination, seamless medium-gray background, crisp inked Korean manhwa character design, controlled 2D cel shading.
```

No añade lluvia, pose heroica, arma, poder, rim light ni escenario.

## 15. Dos hombres similares y un contenedor

```text
The short-haired twenty-four-year-old cleaner in a gray reflective coverall stands completely OUTSIDE the open capsule at screen-left, both boots planted on wet pavement and his shoulders pulled back in alarm. The long-haired bloodied prisoner in torn black clothing, white restraint harness and metal collar is the ONLY person INSIDE the capsule at screen-right, hips and heels supported by the cradle. They lock eyes across a clear one-meter gap, eye-level OTS medium shot from behind the cleaner. Rainy night in the reinforced tunnel; cold capsule light separates both silhouettes. [STYLE ANCHOR].

Si la cápsula se desplaza y el rostro debe ocultarse:

```text
The transparent transport capsule skids across wet pavement with the long-haired restrained prisoner still visible as the ONLY OCCUPANT through rain reflections and backlit silhouette; the tan cradle is never empty. Distant side-profile wide action shot...
```

Nunca elimines la referencia del ocupante dejando el asiento visible.
```

No confía en los nombres. Describe firmas visuales, lados, distancia y quién ocupa el contenedor.

## 16. Emoción observable

Débil:

```text
[PROTAGONIST] looks terrified.
```

Útil:

```text
The short-haired cleaner recoils half a step, eyebrows lifted, eyes fixed on the prisoner, lips parted and both wet hands rigid beside his thighs, eye-level medium reaction shot.
```

Usa al menos dos indicios de rostro/cuerpo. La expresión debe corresponder al verbo y no contradecir la pose de referencia.

## 17. Cadena de estados de criatura

```text
PINNED: The eyeless plated dog lies partly ON TOP of the pavement with its rear body trapped beneath the broken column, foreclaws scraping for leverage and neck twisted toward the workers.

CHARGING: The same pinned dog arches its armored spine, forelegs braced wide and the purple chest seam expanding before the pulse.

IMPACT: The same dog twists airborne away from the column, all four feet off the ground and chest plates bursting outward at the red-black contact point.

AFTERMATH: The same dog lies motionless ON TOP of wet pavement, limbs splayed with clear contacts, chest plates split and purple seam completely dark.
```

Cada estado usa una pose derivada compatible. No reutilices una ficha neutral de pie.
